    Limited Edition Exclusive

    The USA Woollux 504 is a limited edition, website exclusive cap hand-crafted in America from start to finish. This new take on an old classic, made from luxe fibers of the finest merino wool will certainly impress.

    The wool for the USA Woollux 504 Cap comes from merino sheep raised in the Catskills of New York. Our 100% sourced and made in the USA merino wool is a sustainable, harm free fiber harvested in New York, washed in Texas, spun into yarn in Vermont and made into a hat in Pennsylvania by Employee-Owners.

    The USA Woollux 504 Cap features an embroidered Kangol logo and Kangaroo on the back of the cap, along with a comfort stretch sweatband. The Woollux 504 ships in a novelty Kangol hat box and includes a hat brush to keep your hat looking clean and fresh.
