    An iconic Kangol classic, the Tropic 504 is an ivy cap that features a blocked crown with a 2” brim and our Tropic yarn for a lightweight and comfortable fit. The seamless, knitted, and blocked construction has been a beloved staple in our collection since the 1970s. The 504 cap gets its name from the number assigned to the original block shape that it’s made on.

    Stay Sharp in a Classic Cap

    You can wear this ivy tropic hat with just about anything in your wardrobe. You can also wear it any way you like - forward, to the side, or backward in any outdoor event, social event, or even when playing golf. Pick from a wide range of colors to match your casual style. The iconic Tropic 504 cap gives a sense of confidence and a self-assured style that elevates your look. Its low-profile and slimmer design gives it a sharper look with a unique urban flair.

    If you’re looking for an ivy cap with a minimalistic style, the 504 cap is both functional and fashionable. The durability you get out of this cap promises you’ll have your favorite cap for years. Go ahead and order this timeless cap to add the finishing touches to your elegant style from Kangol.
