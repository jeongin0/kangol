Kangol.com Web Accessibility Policy

  • Introduction

    Kangol.com is committed to providing equality of opportunity to persons with disabilities, including equal access to its website.

    This policy is a living document that will change over time as IT changes. Ample opportunities for education and communication will occur whenever changes to the policy take place.

  • Policy Statement

    Kangol commits to ensuring equal access to its website.

    1. · Use web page designs that are consistent with the W3Câ€™s Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.0 (WCAG) Level AA. The goal for Kangol.com is to be reasonably compliant with WCAG 2.0 Level AA.
    2. · Disseminate electronic documents and multimedia on web pages that are consistent with this policy.

  • Applicability

    1. · Official web pages and associated web-based applications.

  • Web Page Requirements

    1. · All new and revised web pages, website templates, and website themes published on or after the effective date of this policy will reasonably comply with WCAG 2.1 Level AA.
    2. · On a periodic basis, Kangol will review its website for compliance with this policy.

  • Complaints

    Please be aware that our efforts are ongoing. Complaints regarding accessibility of Kangol.com Web pages should be directed to accessibility@kangol.com or Phone Number 1.866.852.6465

  • Effective Date

    This policy will take effect as of the date of adoption.
